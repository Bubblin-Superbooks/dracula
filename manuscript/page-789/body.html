Chapter XXIV

Dr. Seward’s Phonograph Diary, Spoken By Van Helsing

THIS to Jonathan Harker.

You are to stay with your dear Madam Mina. We shall go to make our search—if I can call it so, for it is not search but knowing, and we seek confirmation only.
