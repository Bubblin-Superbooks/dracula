Chapter I

Jonathan Harker’s Journal

(Kept in shorthand.)

_3 May. Bistritz._—Left Munich at 8:35 P. M., on 1st May, arriving at Vienna early next morning; should have arrived at 6:46, but train was an hour late. Buda-Pesth seems a wonderful place, from the glimpse which I got of it from the train and the little I could walk through the streets.
